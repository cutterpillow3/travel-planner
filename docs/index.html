How to Use Travel Planner by Mau

  1. You can browse the list of available destinations either in the Destination section below or directly within the Trip Planner section.
  2. You may get more details about a destination by clicking the "View Details" button in the Destination section.
  3. Eco Rating Explained 🌱 Eco Rating per destination is computed by the average rating value of:
    • 🚆 Sustainable Transportation
    • 👣 Low Impact Activities
    • 🛕 Cultural Preservation
    • 🐋 Marine Ecosystem Protection
  4. Once you've decided, you can now use the Trip Planner and select up to 3 destinations of your choice.
  5. Trip Plan results will display the transport options for each destination you've selected, including:
    • 🚢 Boat
    • 🚎 Bus
    • 🚐 Joiner (Van)
    • 🚲 Bicycle
    • 🚶 Walking
    It will also show the average ✨ Eco Rating based on all the destinations you picked. Enjoy & kepp loving Mother Nature! 🌏
